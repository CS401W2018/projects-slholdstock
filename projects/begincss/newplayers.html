Cristiano Ronaldo

Soccer   Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo was born in Madeira, Portugal on February 5, 1985. He grew up in a town where many other kids were playing soccer and quickly found it to be his passion. He played for local teams until eventually signing his first professional contract at the age of 12. He continued to develop his skills and train hard until he became the player with the most professional goals in the history of the sport. As he nears 40 years old and almost 3 decades in the sport, he continues to shine and prove a vital part of the teams he plays for.
