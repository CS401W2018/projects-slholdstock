Mom and Dad

Mary and Brian met in a floral shop more than 25 years ago. Their relationship took off and they have since given birth to 4 kids. They raised them with great values and taught them to work hard and always be goal driven. They often taught about the Savior and how to always have a great relationship with Him. They are almost empty nesters and are trying to figure out what they are going to do with all of this new time that they will have on their hands.

Binx
