This is a tour of some of the largest and prettiest soccer stadiums located in Spain.
Soccer has been played since the late 1800's when British miners introduced the sport.
From then, the sport has taken off in popularity and finds itself deeply rooted in the country's culture.
It was once played on dirt fields and in the streets, but now people travel from all over
to these luxurious stadiums to watch the sport that they love.
